A summary of the dataset.

Upon initial inspection of the data, it is worth noting that the blocked roads (target variable) has a minimum value of zero, implying that the dataset is not entirely comprised of storms which cause blockages. 1-3 roads make up the first and third quartile, so there is a very tight distribution apart from the large maximum. The existence count roads and land use fields indicates that each row comprises a different area. Some very large different between most urban and most rural areas. Percent Conifer can be none or almost a third, supports theory of how urban the area in question is changes a lot even further supported by the almost 0-98% range of Deciduous The most supported by percent developed ranging from 0-1 now I know that look into further details about their distributions. There are many similarities among wind variables. The gust distributions mins and max look consistently similar to the continuous wind. These similarities suggest correlation and will be investigated in the EDA and PCA section. Certain outliers indicate missing values.
